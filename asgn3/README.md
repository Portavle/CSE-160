Cube skybox textured with stars slowly rotating around camera. 
skybox distance is secretly really close to the camera with the world drawing over it (ignores depth buffer).
Cube skybox has a color tint to mimic a milky way band
3 orbitals also orbit around with skybox
Floor is somewhat flat with a little bit of a 'bump' every few units in a grid.
Textbox is a canvas 2d (made for a personal project I made a couple years ago) on top of the main webgl canvas.
Shift and Space to rise and fall
WASD to move camera around on the x and z axis
QE to rotate camera or use the mouse and drag.
